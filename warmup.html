Essential Information: Dynamic Stretches and Proper Warm Ups

Dynamic Stretches

Dynamic stretches are movements that increase your range of motion, improve your circulation, and prepare your muscles for exercise.

Here are some examples of dynamic stretches:
- Leg swings
- Arm circles
- High knees
- Butt kicks
- Lunges with a twist

Proper Warm Ups

Proper warm ups are essential for preventing injury, increasing your performance, and preparing your mind and body for exercise.

Here are some tips for proper warm ups:
- Start with a light cardio activity, such as jogging, cycling, or jumping jacks.
- Gradually increase your intensity and duration.
- Include dynamic stretches to improve your range of motion.
- Focus on the muscles you will be using during your workout.
- Listen to your body and adjust your warm up as needed.
