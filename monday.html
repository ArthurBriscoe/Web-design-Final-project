Monday - Squats + Benchpress

Warm Up

- Treadmill or Stationary Bike: 5-10 minutes
- Dynamic Stretches: leg swings, arm circles, etc.

Exercise 1: Barbell Squats

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 5
- Tempo: 3-0-1
- Rest: 2-3 minutes

- Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your chest up.
- Hold the barbell on your upper back with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
- Lower your body as far as you can by pushing your hips back and bending your knees.
- Pause, then slowly push yourself back to the starting position.

Exercise 2: Front Squats

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 8
- Tempo: 3-0-1
- Rest: 2 minutes

- Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your chest up.
- Hold the barbell in front of your neck with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
- Lower your body as far as you can by pushing your hips back and bending your knees.
- Pause, then slowly push yourself back to the starting position.

Exercise 3: Romanian Deadlifts

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 12
- Tempo: 3-0-1
- Rest: 1-2 minutes

- Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your chest up.
- Hold the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
- Lower the barbell to your shins by pushing your hips back and keeping your knees slightly bent.
- Pause, then slowly push yourself back to the starting position.

Exercise 4: Leg Press

- Sets: 3
- Reps: 12
- Tempo: 3-0-1
- Rest: 1-2 minutes

- Sit in the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart.
- Push the platform away from you by extending your legs.
- Pause, then slowly lower the platform back to the starting position.

Exercise 5: Benchpress

- Top single and 2 sets of 5
- Run through the proper warm up for this compound movement even though you may already feel warm.
- Treat this bench day specifically as a strength day and to see where you're at.
- The percentage for the top single should be around 87-92% of your one rep max.
- Between each warmup make sure to take proper rest time to minimize fatigue.
After completing the top set, document it down in your notes as week 1: Benchpress total and be sure to include the calculated percentage.
